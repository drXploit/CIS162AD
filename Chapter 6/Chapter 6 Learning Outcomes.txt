Explain what is meant by a counter controlled loop,
A counter controlled loop will loop until the counter has hit a certain number that was chosen then it will exit the loop.

What makes a loop a pre-test loop.
A pretest loop will test whether a condition (the body is run first before seeing whether to loop again) has been met before it starts the looping sequence.

What is the definition of an infinite loop?
An infinite loop is a loop that iterates over and over forever because the condition will never be met.

What pre-test loops are available in C#?
while
for
foreach

What post-test loops are available in C#?
do..while
